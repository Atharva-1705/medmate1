What to Do in an Emergency

1. Stay calm and assess the situation.

2. Call the appropriate emergency service listed above.

3. Provide your location and explain the situation clearly.

4. Follow the instructions provided by the emergency personnel.

5. If possible, provide first aid while waiting for professional help to arrive.
